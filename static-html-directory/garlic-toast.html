Ingredients

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F).
  2. Place sliced bread on a baking tray lined with aluminium foil.

  4. Season with garlic salt, to taste.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until crunchy throughout.
