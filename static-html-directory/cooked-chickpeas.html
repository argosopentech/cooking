Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas / garbanzo beans, drained
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oil in pan. Add chickpeas and seasonings.
  2. Cook over medium-high heat for 10-15 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste.
